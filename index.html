Instructions

Hello! Welcome to our COVID-19 Simulator, called Patient Ø. You can utilize sliders to change aspects including mask wearing, social distancing, vaccines/immune, etc. The code will input and reflect the changes, and will display the fact-based simulations. The light blue squares represent the people. The green sections symbolize grass, but people can't walk on grass, and must stay on the pathways which are light brown. The blue rectangles are stores, and the light pink rectangles represent buildings in general. The people will walk around in the town, alternating between various tasks and visiting different buildings. The decisions you make on the sliders will impact the spread of the virus in the population. Good luck!
